弹出框

对于任意自定义的html代码，均可以做弹出框操作。

HTML

<div id="popup" class="panel panel-primary" style="display:none;width:400px">
  <div class="panel-heading">
    <h3 class="panel-title">Panel title</h3>
  </div>
  <div class="panel-body">
    Panel content
  </div>
  <div class="panel-footer text-center">
    <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary" onclick="$('#popup').tHide()">关闭</button>
  </div>
</div>

效果展示

Panel title

Panel content

参数

{
    drag_handle: "",
    modal: false,
    draggableClass: "draggable",
    activeHandleClass: "active-handle"
}

代码

$("#popup").fshow({drag_handle:$(".panel-heading"), modal:true, animate:true})

关闭弹出框

$("#popup").tHide()
